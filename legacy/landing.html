About Me

Here's a little write up about me,
the stuff I like and whatever the fuck else.

I'm Shourya Paruvu.

I'm not very good at being affectionate, or being around people. Sure, I enjoy company for a bit and loneliness eats into me, but it is what it is. I like writing down how I feel in the moment and never sharing it, I keep things to my self and I do shit for my self. I'm arrogant, I'm an asshole, but if I care about you then I really fucking care about you. It's hard to be in the position of constantly being an ass and expecting people to stick around. But oh, what does it really mean? I always want to have friends but I can't really keep them around. It's already too late to go and fix things, and I hope I never come back to this place again. I hope I blink before you do, and when you think of me years down the line you don't have a single nice thing to say about me. I'm drowing, alone, and I've made peace with it. So, it's okay. I hate a lot of things and I can't shut my mouth, love pissing people off. I stand behind my views until I'm proven wrong. But well, fuck it, no one's going to read this might as well say fuck you if you are.

Aviation

My love for aviation startted as a young child, it's all I've ever wanted to do. There's just something so spectacular to watch behind the creation and the theory of a flight. It's one of the best things I'm still optimistic about. My current plan is to get my commercial pilot's license (CPL) from the FAA, come back to India and convert it to a DGCA CPL and fly with a regional airline, like SpiceJet or IndiGo.

Music

I'm really into music, I can't play any instruments but I love the bass. Some of my favourite artists are Radiohead, Eminem, Nine Inch Nails, Mac DeMarco, The Comet is Coming, Beach House, FIDLAR, and The Frights. I mainly listen to alternative rock, surf/garage rock or experimental/math rock. Do send me an email if you've got music you'd think I enjoy.
